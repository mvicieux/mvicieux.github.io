Mittens!






After a lifetime of raising children, the Smith-Erichsen couple is now faced with an empty nest. Rescuing two Siamese cats from unfortunate circumstances, their lives are re-filled with the quirky energy of (fur) babies once again as four BIG personalities become family.

This one-page spread was featured in Issue #9 of The Columbus Scribbler
Mittens! has been archived in the Columbus Metropolitan Library Newspaper Archive
